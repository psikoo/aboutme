> DigiSpark - I'm just a keyboard *wink *wink

- Introduction:
A Digispark is an Attiny85 based microcontroller development board, initially developed and distributed by Digistump LLC. Digistump abandoned the project a couple of years ago and left the task of maintaining the drivers and libraries to the Arduino community.
I came to know of this little and cute USBs [^1] after a friend of my mine showed me one he had been playing around with. I fell in love instantly, and so I bought a couple of them through Amazon to experiment with them.

[^1] so cute and small
